About Us

Technical Workshop Marathon (TWM) is an endeavour by the IEEE Student Branch of Govt. Model Engineering College, Thrikkakara. It is a month long annual event that aims to provide every student with a strong conceptual foundation in the core areas of Electronics, Computer Science and Biomedical Engineering through lectures and hands-on sessions taken by experts from related domains. The event is organised with the idea of sensitising students to newer technologies. TWM 2015 is the sixth edition of the Workshop.

In previous years, the Workshop has enjoyed enthusiastic participation and saw students hugely benefitting from the courses. The experts brought in to conduct the courses were the best from their respective fields and helped make the event a success, both in terms of participation and experience gained.

Workshops conducted covered important fields in the emerging electronics industry. Some of the courses taken up by the TWM participants in previous years include 8051 & PCB Design Basics & PIC, Linux & ARM, Web Development, Circuit Simulators, PCB Design & DSP, HDL, FPGA & VLSI Design, Embedded Controllers, PSoC, etc. These workshops give an opportunity for students to understand the practical aspects along with the theory.

Single Board Computers

Since the coming of the Raspberry Pi Model B, single-board computers (SBCs) have become a prevalent force in the development world.

BeagleBone is a credit-card-sized Linux computer that connects to the Internet and runs software such as Android 4.0 and Ubuntu.

This workshop will cover the essentials of Linux, BeagleBone, and Raspberry Pi and provides Hands on session on BeagleBone.

Fee for IEEE & MEC Students is Rs.150

Signal Processing

Signal processing is an enabling technology that encompasses the fundamental theory, applications, algorithms, and implementations of processing or transferring information contained in many different physical, symbolic, or abstract formats broadly designated as signals.The ability to process them is a vital skill to master them. This workshop will cover the fundamentals of signal processing, as well as a comprehensive lesson on QT Octave and the digital signal processing kit along with it.

Fee for IEEE & MEC Students is Rs.150

FPGA

A field-programmable gate array (FPGA) is an integrated circuit designed to be configured by a customer or a designer after manufacturing. FPGA technology continues to gain momentum and have gone from being simple glue logic chips to actually replacing custom application-specific integrated circuits (ASICs) and processors for signal processing and control applications. Acquire skills in FPGA protyping and VHDL programming. Also get experienced with FPGA starter kit through this informative workshop.

Fee for IEEE & MEC Students is Rs.150

8051 Microntrollers

Microcontrollers help shape systems all over the world to enable design advancements that are fast, green, smart and fun. Learn and explore the benefits of microcontrollers and design your dream projects using 8051 microcontroller.

Fee for IEEE & MEC Students is Rs.150

EDA TOOLS

Electronic design automation (EDA) is a category of software tools for designing electronic systems such as printed circuit boards and integrated circuits. The tools work together in a design flow that chip designers use to design and analyze entire semiconductor chips. Learn about circuit simulation using NG SPICE through this informative workshop with a special focus on PCB design using EAGLE

Fee for IEEE & MEC Students is Rs.150
